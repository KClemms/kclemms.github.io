THOMAS TALLIS

(born c. 1505-1585)

Thomas Tallis was one of the most important composers of Sacred Music. His style of music focused on the Reformation and the advancement of polyphonic schools. He is known as both an organist and a singer in the Canterbury Cathedral.

His Most Famous Works

  • Lamentations
  • Discomfort Them, Oh Lord
  • O Sacrum Convivum
  • Audivi Vocem
