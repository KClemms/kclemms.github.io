Johann Sebastian Bach

(1685-1750)

Johann Sebastian Bach was a genius composer and organ improviser, born in Germany to a family of musicians. Considered one of the greatest composers of all times, Bach's was said to embody the past, present and future of classical music.

Famous Works

  • The Toccata and Fugue in D minor
  • BWV 578, Fugue in D minor
  • Ave Maria
  • Brandengurg Concertos
  • Air on the G string
  • Goldenburg Variations
