Vivaldi

(1678-1741)

Antonio Vivaldi was a famous composer and musician that held many jobs. Born in Italy, Vivaldi was a composer, violist, teacher and a cleric. He is viewed as one of the greatest and most influential composers of the Baroque era.

Famous Works

  • Four Seasons
  • Stabat Mater
  • Magnificat
  • Gloria
  • Mandolin Concerto
  • Six Flute Concertos, Op. 10
