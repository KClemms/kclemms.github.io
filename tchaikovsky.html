TCHAIKOVSKY

(1840-1893)

Pyotr llyich Tchaikovsky was a Russian composer. He always held an interest in music but did not become a serious composer until the age of 14. Tchaikovsky was the first Russian composer whose music made a lasting impression in the genre of classical music.

Famous Works

  • Swan Lake
  • The Seasons
  • Symphony No. 6
  • Piano Concerto No. 1
  • Violin Concerto
  • 1812 Overture
  • Romeo and Juliet
  • The Nutcracker
